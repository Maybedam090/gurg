How to play

  1. Login with Google from the home page.
  2. Watch your cash balance and portfolio update as you simulate trades.
  3. Save your progress anytime by clicking the Save button.
  4. Return later and your account will restore your balance.

Game rules

This demo gives you virtual cash to invest. Each simulated trade changes your cash and portfolio. The goal is to grow your account by making smart trades and preserving your balance.

Admin panel

If you are the admin, open the Admin page after logging in with your assigned Google account. The admin panel shows all accounts and lets you reset progress.
